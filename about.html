Our Story

Founded on the principle that artists should keep 100% of their rights, VoicePawa is a digital music distribution platform that connects artists, labels, and brands to major streaming platforms worldwide.

We specialize in simple, transparent distribution with no upfront submission payments. Beyond distribution, we offer playlist support, social media integration, PR services, and comprehensive brand management.
